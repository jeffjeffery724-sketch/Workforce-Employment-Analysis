
# Workforce & Employment Analysis Dashboard

This project is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze workforce demographics, employment status, AI risk exposure, industry distribution, salary levels, and age/location trends. The dashboard helps visualize large-scale workforce data and provides valuable insights for HR analytics, organizational planning, and labor market research.

---

## 📌 Project Objective
The objective of this dashboard is to transform workforce-related data into meaningful visual insights. It highlights employment patterns, salary averages, industry distribution, and demographic trends while enabling users to explore data interactively through filters.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **2000 Total Records**
- **$76.89K Average Salary**
- **15 Years Average Experience**

These metrics provide a high-level overview of the dataset’s scale and workforce characteristics.

---

## 🔹 Employment Status
### **Employed vs Unemployed**
- Employed: **885**
- Unemployed: **847**

A nearly even split suggests a balanced but competitive job landscape.

---

## 🔹 Employment & Education Trend
Education-level distribution shows how individuals are spread across qualifications:

- **High School** – Highest representation  
- **PhD** – Second highest  
- **Masters / Diploma / Bachelor’s** – Moderate representation  

This indicates a mix of both highly educated and moderately educated individuals.

## 🔹 Industry Distribution

Retail dominates the dataset, showing higher workforce concentration.

Urban centers dominate the dataset.

## 🔧 Dashboard Filters

- Employment Status

- Month

- Industry  

---

## 📈 Insights Summary
- Workforce distribution spans all age groups, locations, and education levels.
- Employment ratio is nearly balanced between employed and unemployed individuals.
- Average salary and years of experience suggest a mid-level to senior-level workforce.
- Retail is the most represented industry, followed by healthcare and technology.
- High AI-risk roles are prominent, indicating future automation concerns.
- Urban regions dominate the employment landscape.

---

## 🏁 Conclusion
This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth view of workforce patterns, employment status, industry behavior, AI risk exposure, and demographic insights. It can be used by HR teams, policymakers, and analysts to understand workforce readiness, identify risks, and support data-driven decision-making.
